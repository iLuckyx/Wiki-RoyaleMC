# Automatic Ignition Chamber

## Description

The Automatic Ignition Chamber is used to keep the fire underneath the [Smeltery](smeltery.md) and [Makeshift Smeltery](makeshift-smeltery.md) lit, eliminating the need to manually light the fire when it is consumed while crafting.

![Automatic Ignition Chamber](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (191).png>)

## Crafting & Recipe

**Table:** [Enhanced Crafting Table](enhanced-crafting-table.md)\
**Recipe:**\
\- x1 Flint and Steel\
\- x1 [Basic Circuit Board](../technical-components/basic-circuit-board.md)\
\- x1 Observer\
\- x4 Iron Ingot

## Usage

Place the Automatic Ignition Chamber touching the Dispenser of either the [Smeltery](smeltery.md) or [Makeshift Smeltery](makeshift-smeltery.md), and then fill it with flint and steels. The Smeltery or Makeshift Smeltery still needs a fire source underneath it.&#x20;
